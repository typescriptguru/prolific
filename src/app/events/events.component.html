About Us

Prolific is a national non-profit organization that is dedicated and determined to helping change our youth’s overall health and well-being.

We are committed to providing them with the tools, inspiration, education and knowledge that they need to create a bright future. How are we planning on doing that? By focusing on four major areas: nutrition, fitness, community and inspiration. We feel strongly that focusing in on and helping our youth in those four specific areas will produce incredible results and create healthy habits that will last a lifetime.

It’s no secret that Americans are currently experiencing a wide variety of health challenges.

From obesity, to diabetes to an increase in children with autism, we are at a serious crossroads. We can’t just sit back and let this happen, Prolific feels a sense of urgency to help get our youth’s health back on track. Prolific is diving in and taking the lead to encourage and show children how important being healthy, fit and happy is to creating a successful life. We know this won’t happen overnight but with your help and your constant dedication, together we will be what our youth needs to turn things around for the better.
